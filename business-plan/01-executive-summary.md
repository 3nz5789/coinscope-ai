## CoinScopeAI — One-page summary

> **AI-driven capital-preservation infrastructure that enforces the discipline you've already built.**

### The thesis

Three structural shifts are converging inside a 24–36 month window. Surviving disciplined retail crypto-perp traders rejected the signal-group playbook and now buy *process* over *signals* (Force 1 — demand). AI collapsed the cost of building institutional-grade quant tools — a small team can ship what required hedge-fund engineering in 2019 (Force 2 — supply). MENA has moved from downstream market to crypto-native infrastructure hub, with regulatory clarity and growing family-office allocation (Force 3 — geographic foothold). Defensible share is at the intersection; the window closes as Force 2 brings competitors online.

### The problem

Disciplined retail and small-fund crypto-perp traders have built their own risk frameworks; the tools they're offered either replace those frameworks or ignore them entirely. Manual discipline breaks at scale, and the cost of failure is account capital.

### The product

CoinScopeAI is an AI-driven engine that evaluates signals across USDT-perpetual venues, classifies market regime in real time (Trending / Mean-Reverting / Volatile / Quiet), and runs configurable risk gates (drawdown, daily-loss, leverage, position heat) at user-defined thresholds before any trade arms. Capital stays in the user's exchange account. Three tiers — **Free / Trader ($79/mo) / Desk** (Preview at v1, Full at v2) — with per-seat scaling at Desk Full.

What we explicitly do not do: alpha generation, signals delivery, autonomous execution without user authorization, capital custody, fund-formation tooling.

### Where we are

Active 30-day validation phase against documented Capital Cap criteria (P0, May 2026). Soft launch (P1) opens June 1, 2026 — 40 paid users at founder-cohort pricing. Public launch (P2) target August–September 2026, gate-driven. Three-persona ICP (Methodist / Engineer / Solo PM) is locked at v1 with §3.7 interview validation in flight. P1 vendor stack narrow (CCXT, CoinGlass, Tradefeeds, CoinGecko, Claude minimal). Engine live on Binance Testnet only — no real capital during validation.

### What the numbers say

Base-case M24 ARR ~$1.9M (range: bear $1.0M / base $1.9M / bull $3.0M). Gross margin ~76% base. LTV/CAC at base CAC: Trader 13×, Desk Preview 17×, Desk Full 26× (5–10× under founder-time-honest CAC). Payback under 3 months across all tiers. North-star: MAVT (Monthly Active Validated Traders), paired with ARR for the financial story.

### Why us

UAE-resident founder, sole prop, MENA-rooted by design. Engineering through validation; P4 contractor support (~3 months at v2 build) at the highest-risk phase. Anti-overclaim discipline applied to capital allocation — we don't claim production-ready until §8 Capital Cap criteria are met, and we don't claim ARR we haven't earned.

### The ask

Pre-validation: warm conversations only. No priced equity raise before validation passes. We're building relationships for the post-validation conversation. If you'd like to be on the list when cohort data lands, here's the cadence.

Post-validation: structured raise opens with cohort data as centerpiece. Use of funds: 60–70% engineering (P4 contractor scenario + extended runway), 20–25% GTM scaling (paid acquisition trigger M5+ if Trader CAC validates), 10–15% operations (legal entity restructure, vendor contracts, soft-cohort to paid-cohort transition).
