## 2026-05-01 §5 — Trader full + Desk Preview at v1; full Desk at v2

**Decision:** v1 ships **Trader tier full** and a **Desk Preview tier**. Full Desk tier (multi-account aggregation, audit-grade exports, role-based seats, LP-style book-level gates) is a v2 commitment.

**Trader tier v1:** built around the six engine API endpoints (VTN) plus dashboard IB capabilities labeled as "stabilizing" rather than "shipped." Serves P1 (Methodist) fully and P2 (Engineer) base layer.

**Desk Preview tier v1:** subset of Desk features at IB-or-higher maturity — primarily the performance journal UI configured for multi-account view-only, with single-PM access. Persona 3 onboards but does not get partner seats, audit-grade exports, or LP-style gates until v2. Explicitly labeled "Preview" in product copy and pricing.

**Full Desk tier v2:** ships when multi-account dashboard, audit-grade journal export, role-based access (seats), and tax-ready exports cross IB → VTN. Persona 3 paid acquisition opens at full Desk launch; pre-V2 Persona 3 users join a waitlist or use Desk Preview.

**SUPERSEDED (in part) by §5.4 roadmap-review entry:** Tax-ready exports and LP-style book-level gates were originally listed as v2 commitments here. The §5.4 refinement pass deferred both to v3. v2 Desk Full now ships with multi-account + audit-grade exports + role-based seats only. See the §5.4 entry for current truth.

**Why this over Trader-only or Push-Desk-to-v1:**

- Trader-only would delay Persona 3 paid acquisition entirely, losing 12+ months of high-ARPU revenue runway.
- Push-Desk-to-v1 requires accelerating multiple IB/RM items inside the validation window — high engineering risk with the validation phase active.
- C honors the "we'll be back for you" stance applied to Persona 3 features, gives v1 a defensible Desk-shaped offering, and avoids overclaim by labeling Preview as Preview.

**Cascades into:** §5.2 tier matrix (3-tier structure: Free / Trader / Desk Preview; full Desk on v2 roadmap), §6 pricing (Desk Preview priced lower than full Desk would be, with v2 upgrade pathway), §7 GTM (Persona 3 acquisition strategy split between waitlist + Preview cohort), §11 financial model (Desk-tier revenue ramps in v2, not v1), §14 launch (Desk Preview cohort metrics monitored separately).
